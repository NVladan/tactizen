Getting Started

Tactizen uses Web3 wallet authentication - no traditional account creation needed!

  1. Install MetaMask or any Web3-compatible wallet browser extension
  2. Click "Connect Wallet" on the Tactizen homepage
  3. Approve the connection in your wallet
  4. Sign the authentication message (this proves you own the wallet)
  5. Create your citizen profile with a username

That's it! Your wallet address is your account - no passwords to remember.

A Web3 wallet (like MetaMask) is a secure digital wallet that:

  • Stores your blockchain credentials securely
  • Allows you to sign in without passwords
  • Enables you to own NFTs and in-game assets
  • Lets you participate in blockchain-verified voting

We recommend MetaMask for beginners - it's free and easy to set up.

When creating your citizen, you'll be asked to choose a country. Consider:

  • Population: Larger countries have more active communities
  • Resources: Different regions have different natural resources
  • Economy: Check the job market and company presence
  • Politics: Some countries are more politically active

Don't worry too much - you can always travel or change citizenship later!

Gameplay

Daily activities to maximize your progress:

  • Train: Increases your strength (important for battles)
  • Study: Improves your knowledge and skills
  • Work: Earn currency at your job
  • Check Missions: Complete daily missions for bonus rewards
  • Vote: Participate in any active elections

Most actions have daily limits that reset at midnight (server time).

There are several ways to earn currency:

  • Work for a company: Get a job and work daily for wages
  • Start your own company: Hire workers and sell products
  • Trade on the marketplace: Buy low, sell high
  • Complete missions: Daily and weekly missions offer rewards
  • Political positions: Government roles come with salaries
  • Military bounties: Complete bounty missions for rewards

Combat occurs in active wars between countries:

  • Go to the Wars page to see active battles
  • Choose a side and click Fight
  • Your damage depends on your strength and equipment
  • Each fight costs energy (regenerates over time)
  • Battles have rounds - the side with most damage wins

Join a military unit for coordinated attacks and better rewards!

Economy & Companies

To create a company:

  1. Go to My Places → Companies
  2. Click "Create Company"
  3. Choose the industry type (food, weapons, raw materials, etc.)
  4. Select a region with appropriate resources
  5. Pay the creation fee

You'll need to hire workers and buy raw materials to produce goods. Location matters - some regions have resource bonuses!

Tactizen has multiple currencies:

  • Local Currency: Each country has its own currency (earned from jobs, used in local marketplace)
  • ZEN: Premium currency for special items and features
  • Gold: Universal trading currency

You can exchange currencies on the Currency Market page.

Politics & Voting

There are two types of elections:

  • Presidential: One leader is elected per country. Candidates run individually.
  • Congressional: Multiple seats filled based on party votes. Join a party to run!

Elections have phases: Registration → Voting → Results. Check the Elections page to see what's active.

Tactizen uses advanced cryptography for voting:

  • Your vote is secret: No one can see who you voted for
  • Verified on blockchain: The zkVerify blockchain confirms your vote is valid
  • No double voting: Cryptographic nullifiers prevent voting twice
  • Transparent yet private: Anyone can verify the election is fair without seeing individual votes

This is real democratic technology - your vote truly matters and is truly secret!

Technical Issues

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Make sure MetaMask (or your wallet) is installed and unlocked
  2. Refresh the page and try again
  3. Check if your browser blocks pop-ups (wallet needs to open)
  4. Try a different browser (Chrome works best with MetaMask)
  5. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  6. Disable other wallet extensions that might conflict

Still having issues? Submit a support ticket.

Common reasons for failed actions:

  • Insufficient funds: Check if you have enough currency
  • Daily limit reached: Some actions have daily caps
  • Low energy: Combat and work require energy
  • Session expired: Try refreshing the page and reconnecting
  • Server maintenance: Check for announcements

Account & Safety

We take security seriously:

  • Web3 authentication - no passwords to steal
  • Session fingerprinting to detect suspicious activity
  • All connections are encrypted (HTTPS)
  • We never store or access your private keys

Your security depends on keeping your wallet seed phrase safe. Never share it with anyone!
