Introduction

Tactizen Recreation ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use Tactizen ("the Game").

02

Information We Collect

Wallet Information

When you connect your Web3 wallet, we collect:

  • Your public wallet address
  • Transaction signatures for authentication

We never have access to your private keys.

Profile Information

Information you voluntarily provide:

  • Username
  • Avatar image
  • Bio/description
  • In-game preferences
Game Activity Data

We automatically collect:

  • Game actions (training, working, voting, etc.)
  • IP addresses and device information
  • Session data and timestamps
User-Generated Content

Content you voluntarily create:

  • Newspaper articles and publications
  • Private messages to other players
  • Comments and public posts

Important: We do not actively monitor or review user-generated content. You are solely responsible for the content you create and share. We are not liable for content posted by users.

Blockchain Data

When you use blockchain features:

  • NFT ownership and transfers
  • ZK proof verifications (anonymous voting)
  • Smart contract interactions
03

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

Provide and maintain the Game

Authenticate your identity

Process in-game transactions

Prevent cheating and abuse

Communicate with you about the Game

Improve our services

04

Zero-Knowledge Voting Privacy

Your Vote is Secret

Our voting system uses zkSNARK technology to ensure:

Vote Privacy

Your vote choice is never revealed

Eligibility Proof

Only proof that you're an eligible voter is recorded

Double-Vote Prevention

Cryptographic nullifiers prevent voting twice without revealing identity

Blockchain Verification

Proofs are verified on zkVerify blockchain for transparency

This means your voting record shows that you voted, but not how you voted.

05

Data Sharing

We do NOT sell your personal information.

We may share data with:

  • Blockchain Networks: Transaction data is publicly visible on blockchain explorers
  • Service Providers: Hosting, analytics, and security services that help us operate
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
06

Data Security

We implement security measures including:

Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
Session security with fingerprinting
Rate limiting to prevent abuse
Regular security audits

However, no system is 100% secure. Please protect your wallet credentials.

07

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion:

  • Profile data is removed or anonymized
  • Game statistics may be retained in aggregate form
  • Blockchain data cannot be deleted (immutable by design)
08

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

Access your personal data
Correct inaccurate data
Request deletion of your data
Object to certain processing
Data portability

Contact us through our Support System to exercise these rights.

09

Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies for:

  • Session management
  • Security features
  • Remembering preferences

We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

10

Children's Privacy

Tactizen is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

11

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through in-game announcements. Continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us
