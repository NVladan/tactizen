What is Tactizen?

Tactizen is a blockchain-powered nation simulation game where players become citizens of virtual countries, participating in economics, politics, military conflicts, and social activities.

Built with Web3 technology, Tactizen offers a unique gaming experience that combines strategic gameplay with true digital ownership and revolutionary privacy-preserving voting technology.

Core Features

Political System

Run for office, vote in elections, and shape the destiny of your nation. Our revolutionary Zero-Knowledge Voting ensures anonymous and tamper-proof elections.

Economy & Business

Start companies, employ workers, trade goods, and participate in the dynamic marketplace. The economy reflects real supply and demand dynamics.

Military & Combat

Join military units, participate in wars, and defend your country's interests. Strategic battles determine territorial control and national prestige.

Community & Social

Form political parties, publish newspapers, join military units, and build alliances. Collaboration and diplomacy are key to success.

NFT Integration

Own unique in-game assets as NFTs. Trade equipment, special items, and collectibles with true digital ownership.

Privacy-First Voting

zkSNARK proofs verified on zkVerify blockchain ensure your vote is secret while maintaining election integrity.

Technology Stack

Web3 Authentication

Connect with MetaMask or any compatible wallet - no passwords needed

zkSNARK Voting

Groth16 zero-knowledge proofs for completely anonymous voting

zkVerify Blockchain

On-chain proof verification for transparent yet private elections

NFT Marketplace

ERC-721 smart contracts for true asset ownership
