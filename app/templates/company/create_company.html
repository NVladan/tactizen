Company Types Explained
Raw Material Production
  • Mining: Extracts Coal, Iron Ore, Stone
  • Resource Extraction: Harvests Clay, Oil, Sand

These companies produce more units at higher quality levels and employ more workers. Can optionally use Electricity for +30% production boost.

Weapon Manufacturing
  • Rifle Company: Produces Rifles (requires Iron Bar + Coal)

Food & Beverages
  • Bakery: Produces Bread (requires Wheat)
  • Brewery: Produces Beer (requires Wheat)
  • Winery: Produces Wine (requires Grape)
Other Manufacturing
  • Semi-Product: Manufactures Steel, Iron Bar (Iron Ore + Coal), Bricks (Clay + Coal), Concrete (Stone + Sand), Electricity (Oil)
  • Construction: Builds Houses, Forts, Hospitals (requires Bricks + Concrete)

Manufacturing companies produce quality items (Q1-Q5). Higher quality requires more input resources but produces better products. Can optionally use Electricity for +30% production boost.
