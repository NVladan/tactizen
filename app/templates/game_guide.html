Game Guide

Master every aspect of Tactizen with this comprehensive mechanics guide

Welcome to Tactizen

Tactizen is a strategic browser-based nation simulation game where you build your character, participate in politics, manage companies, and fight in battles. The game combines economic simulation, political strategy, and military combat with blockchain integration.

Getting Started

Choose Citizenship
Select your country to start your journey. Your citizenship affects which parties you can join and elections you can participate in.
Train Daily
Train every 23 hours to improve your military skills. Higher skills mean more damage in battle.
Get a Job
Work at companies to earn currency and improve your work skills. Check the Job Market for available positions.
Join Battles
Fight for your country in active battles. Use weapons to maximize damage and earn military rank XP.

Combat System

Damage Formula

Your damage in battle is calculated using multiple factors:

Base Damage = (Player Level x 2) + (Skill x 10)

Final Damage = Base Damage x (1 + (Rank Bonus% + Weapon Bonus% + NFT Bonus%) / 100)

Weapon Bonuses

Equip weapons before fighting to increase your damage output:

Helicopter (Aviation) +30% +60% +90% +120% +150%

Battle Structure

  • Battles last 24 hours divided into 3 rounds (8 hours each)
  • Win a battle by winning 2 out of 3 rounds
  • Three wall types: Infantry, Armoured, and Aviation
  • Each fight costs 10 Energy and 10 Wellness
  • Cooldown between fights: 1.5 seconds

Regional Constructions

The President or Minister of Defense can place hospitals and fortresses in regions under their country's control. Only one of each type can exist per region.

Hospital
Allows defenders and their allies to restore wellness during active battles. Players must fight at least once to use the hospital.
Fortress
Provides defensive advantage by reducing attacker damage in battles fought in that region.

Destruction on Conquest
When a region is conquered by another nation, all constructions are destroyed. Hospitals and fortresses cannot be removed manually - they are permanent until the region changes ownership.

Military Ranks

Progress through 60 military ranks from Recruit to Field Marshal. Earn rank XP by dealing damage in battles.

Rank XP Earned = Damage Dealt x 0.1

With Military Tutor NFT: Rank XP x (1 + NFT Bonus% / 100)
Pro Tip
Higher ranks provide increasing damage bonuses. A Q5 Military Tutor NFT doubles your rank XP gain!

Production System

Production Points (PP)

When you work at a company, you generate Production Points based on your skill level:

Base PP = Hours Worked x (1 + Skill Level)

With Electricity: PP x 1.3 (+30% boost)

With Production NFT: Final PP x (1 + NFT Bonus% / 100)

  • Iron Ore - Refined into steel for weapons and construction
  • Stone - Building material for construction

Political System

Political Parties

  • Create a party for 5 Gold
  • Parties are country-specific
  • Party President is elected by members
  • Members can run for government positions

Presidential Elections

Nominations
1st - 5th of each month, 9 AM CET
Voting
5th - 6th of each month, 9 AM CET

The President can appoint ministers and propose laws to Congress.

Congressional Elections

Applications
1st - 25th of each month, 9 AM CET
Voting
25th - 26th of each month, 9 AM CET

Congress members vote on laws proposed by the President.

Government Positions

  • President - Head of state, proposes laws, appoints ministers
  • Congress - Legislative body, votes on laws
  • Minister of Finance - Manages economy, taxes, currency
  • Minister of Defence - Military affairs, bounty contracts
  • Minister of Foreign Affairs - Alliances, diplomacy

Law Types

  • War Declaration - Declare war on another country
  • Trade Embargo - Restrict trade with a country
  • Military Budget - Allocate funds for defense
  • Currency Printing - Issue new currency
  • Tax Changes - Modify tax rates
  • Alliance - Form alliances with other nations
  • Impeachment - Remove the President from office

Bounty Contracts

Military units can accept bounty contracts from Ministers of Defence:

  • Contracts specify a target damage amount in a battle
  • Unit members contribute to the damage goal
  • Successful completion rewards the unit treasury
  • Failed contracts may have penalties

NFT System

NFT Basics

  • Players can equip up to 3 NFTs
  • Q1 NFTs can be purchased for 1 ZEN token
  • Higher quality NFTs obtained by burning 3:1 (3 Q1 = 1 Q2, etc.)
  • Companies have NFT slots based on quality (Q1-Q2: 1, Q3-Q4: 2, Q5: 3)

Economy

Currencies

  • Gold - Global premium currency, used for company creation, party creation, etc.
  • Local Currency - Each country has its own currency for local trade
  • ZEN Token - Blockchain token for NFT purchases

Marketplace

Buy and sell resources on the marketplace:

  • List items at your desired price
  • Market prices fluctuate based on supply and demand
  • Quality affects resource value
  • Currency exchange available for international trade

Working & Wages

Worker Payment = Production Points Generated x Wage Per PP

Company owners set the wage rate. Higher skilled workers generate more PP and earn more.

Travel

  • Base travel cost: 1 Gold or 50 Energy
  • Travel Discount NFT reduces or eliminates costs
  • Q5 Travel Discount = Free travel

Storage

  • Base storage limit: 1000 items
  • Storage Increase NFT: +1000 to +5000 additional slots
  • Maximum with Q5 NFT: 6000 items
